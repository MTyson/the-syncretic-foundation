The Pathless Way

Syncretism means finding unity without demanding uniformity. We believe that every spiritual tradition is a unique language attempting to describe the same unnamable source.


Our foundation provides a space where the many ways of expressing the inexpressible can come together. The Cross, the Om, the Crescent, even the simple, sacred question mark. We are non-dogmatic, non-denominational, and fiercely inclusive.


"The Pathless Way" means that even when we are firmly on the trail, there is a mysterious wildness. Even when we are completely lost, there is a hidden trail.
